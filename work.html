What I Do

Currently Cody works exclusively as a Competitive Coach, Choreographer and Instructor for Surge Seattle. Cody’s competitive repertoire consists of solos, duos and small groups in age divisions mini through senior in skill divisions novice through elite. Cody’s competitive coaching/choreography credits include multiple top 10’s including Novice Mini solo 1st, 2nd and 3rd Overall and Dancer of the Year nominee. Cody offers classes, coaching, choreography and private lessons for dancers Mini (age 6-8) through Adult in divisions novice through elite.
